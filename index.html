About

Words that work as hard as you do.

I'm Corinna Underwood — a freelance SEO copywriter and content strategist with over 25 years of experience building brand voices that resonate, convert, and endure. My background in Philosophy and Women's Studies gives me an analytical edge: I don't just write, I think through what your audience needs to hear and why.

Whether you need a single landing page or a full content strategy, I bring craft and rigour to every word.
